Release notes: TankToys v0.0.1


Features:

  • Tank Battles: Engage in epic battles with your friends using adorable toy tanks.
  • Map Variety: Explore different environments with unique challenges and terrains.
  • Power-ups: Collect power-ups to gain advantages and turn the tide of battle.
  • Customization: Personalize your tank with cool skins and accessories.

Improvements:

  • Enhanced graphics for a more immersive gaming experience.
  • Improved matchmaking for smoother and fairer gameplay.
  • Tweaked controls for better responsiveness.
